Glossary

Pulse :

Project that explores the evolution of ICDs (Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators) and the human heart.

MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions):

A university department focused on skills related to multimedia, the web, and digital communication.

Heart rate :

The number of heartbeats per minute, often used as an indicator of health.

Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD):

A medical device that regulates heart rhythm by delivering electrical impulses in case of arrhythmia.

Emotional music :

Compositions designed to evoke specific emotions in the listener.

Sensory Immersion:

An experience where multiple senses are engaged to create a more intense and immersive interaction.

Symphony :

Composition musicale complexe, souvent utilisée comme métaphore pour décrire une harmonie d’éléments divers.

Collaboration :

Teamwork where several people contribute to a common project.

Visual Interaction:

The process of integrating visual elements that respond to other stimuli, such as music, to create an immersive experience.

Creative Project:

An initiative aimed at creating something innovative, generally artistic or technical.
