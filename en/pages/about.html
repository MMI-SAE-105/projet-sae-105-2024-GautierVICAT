About Pulse

Logo of Pulse

Hello, I am Gautier Vicat, and I am in my first year of a Bachelor's degree in Multimedia and Internet (BUT MMI) in Montbéliard. Pulse is the result of a project themed “Prehistory of the Future!” This site showcases various artistic works such as films, books, and music, all centered around the same subject defined by the quote presented on the homepage. For my site, the initial theme is the Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) and the concepts of life and death related to the human heart. I have approached this theme to discuss different perspectives on the challenges that medicine faced a few years ago in effectively distributing appropriate care. I specifically highlighted the evolution of ICDs.
