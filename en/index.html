Pulse: At the Heart of Technology and Life

Image representing a human heart and an AI-generated DAI

Welcome to this site dedicated to implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs), devices that today save lives by restoring heartbeats at the boundary between life and death. In addition to exploring the fascinating history of these technologies, we also look to the future of ICDs. Imagine devices that are even smaller, smarter, and more connected, capable of anticipating and preventing heart attacks before they even occur. Through films, music, books, and the visions of filmmakers, we invite you to explore not only the human heart but also the medical advancements of tomorrow, where science could redefine our relationship with life and death.
